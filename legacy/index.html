How it works

You tell us what happened. We help you sort it out. We keep it calm and we keep it in order. If you need a professional, we tell you plainly and help you prepare.

1) We figure out what this is

best fit

Many problems sound the same at first. We look for the signals that matter: deadlines, missing documents, who is demanding what, and what happens next in cases like yours.

See modules →

2) We warn you before you get hurt

stop list

People get into trouble by acting too fast, replying the wrong way, or waiting too long. We tell you what not to do yet, so you don’t make your situation worse.

Read “what this is” →

3) You get a short list for today

1–3 steps

You will not get a long checklist. You’ll get the next few steps that actually move the situation forward. Then we continue from there.

Start now →

4) We help you stay steady

no surprises

When the next email or notice arrives, it should not take your breath away. The system can prepare you for what’s likely coming so you’re ready when it shows up.
